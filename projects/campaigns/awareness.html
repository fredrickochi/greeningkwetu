Tips to help protect our planet
Three simple things you can do to help protect our planet.

1. Use long-lasting light bulbs. Energy efficient light bulbs reduce greenhouse gas emissions. Also flip the light switch off when you leave the room!

2. Reduce, reuse, and recycle. Cut down on what you throw away. Follow the three "R's" to conserve natural resources and landfill space.

3. Plant a tree. Trees provide food and oxygen. They help save energy, clean the air, and help combat climate change.

Deforestation
Deforestation has negative effects on the environment and is now most rampant in the Amazon forest, an essential geographical feature of the planet that helps in regulating global weather patterns.

The loss of trees and other vegetation brings about climate change, soil erosion, desertification, food insecurity, flooding, and increased greenhouse emissions in the atmosphere.
